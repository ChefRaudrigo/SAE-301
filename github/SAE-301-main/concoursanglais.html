The George Meliés Fantastic Short Film Festival is organizing a Trial !

Rules are simples :
Product, realize and edit a short-film and send it to us.
20 shorts-films will be presented during the festival
and will be submitted to a vote at the end.

There will be one winner by short-film type
and the votes will be based on differents criterias.
