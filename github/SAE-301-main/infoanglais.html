Information sur le Festival.

What is le Fantastique du Velay ?

Fantastic short film festival, in homage to Georges Méliès.

This short film festival takes place every year and begins this year with the theme of fantasy. For this first time, we find distinguished guests with two great French directors who give us the pleasure of being the sponsors of the event, we will of course find actors as well as influencers. For this first edition, we will honor Georges Méliès, a great name in cinema since he is one of the precursors in the genre of fantasy in cinema.

Information general

When will be the event ?
The festival will take place from 04/21/2023 to 04/23/2023, and will start at 6:30 p.m. on Friday and end on Saturday at 10:30 p.m.
The program is present on the home page of our website if you would like more information.
Where is the Festival ?
Le Fantastique du Velay will take place at the Puy-En-Velay's theatre.
Adress : Pl. du Breuil, 43000 Le Puy-en-Velay

Participate

How to participate ?
To participate in this Short Film Festival, all you have to do is create an account and book your tickets on our website, you can choose the days you want to go and you will find all the programs planned for the festival on our website.
How to register a movie ?
For this first edition, we made the decision to select the short films upstream (before 04/14/2023), an announcement was made on our Instagram account @Fantastique_du_Velay which redirects to our email to then make a selection.
For the next edition, the organization will ensure that you can give your film before the start of the event to enjoy all the creations.
Thanks for your understanding.
